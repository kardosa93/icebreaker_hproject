Social Media – Games


The use of games in social media ecosystems is well established as demonstrated by games such as Farmville. These games have a symbiotic relationship with the related platform with game performance and participation integrated into the social media stream. This project will involve the development of a social media related game which would act as an ‘ice breaker’ allowing users of a private social media platform to get to know one and another.


The project will involve carrying out a literature review into social media games see starter articles at:
